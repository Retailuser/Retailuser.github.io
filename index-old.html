Operational Blueprint

Capture-Validate-Sync-Report

Structured system flow guarantees flawless real-time updates and complete integrity of audit sheets.

01

Register & Vet

Staff submit registry requests. Accounts remain pending in system lock until manual approval by an administrator.

02

Surfacing Priorities

Workers review items grouped by Penang regions. Unchecked pending assets are immediately prioritized at the top of the queue.

03

Tag & Record

Enter the room level, size or model, and condition remarks. Attach the tagging sticker photo and an asset photo for each item.

04

Excel Mother File Sync

Export the updated Penang asset list back to CSV in the same column format (A through AI).
